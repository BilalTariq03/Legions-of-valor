LEGIONS OF VALOR - AI BOT UPDATE
=================================

This build adds a solo mode from the title screen.

New title-screen option:
- Play vs AI
- Choose your faction
- Choose the bot faction
- Choose difficulty: Easy, Normal, or Hard
- Click Start AI Duel

How the AI works:
- The AI sits in Player 2.
- It uses the same game rules/reducer as a human player.
- It chooses Battleplans, deploys units, equips cards, sets traps on higher difficulties, attacks, ends phases, and answers Parry prompts.

Difficulty notes:
- Easy: weaker Battleplan choices, fewer parries, basic attacks.
- Normal: better Battleplan choices, basic parry logic, cautious strikes into hidden back rows.
- Hard: stronger Battleplan scoring, better parry decisions, better deployment and target selection.

Important:
- This is still an MVP training bot, not a perfect competitive AI.
- The bot is meant to let you test flow, balance, card feel, and basic gameplay without needing a second laptop.
